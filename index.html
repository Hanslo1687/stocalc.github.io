Stochastic Calculus and Mathematical Finance

Taught by Prof. Sandeep Juneja, Ashoka University

About the Course

This is a course for advanced undergraduate students with an aptitude and interest in quantitative methods. Students will be exposed to no-arbitrage based deep and beautiful ideas underlying mathematical finance. For this, they will gain adequate exposure to stochastic calculus. Stochastic calculus is also highly relevant for the increasingly mainstream diffusion-based generative AI.
